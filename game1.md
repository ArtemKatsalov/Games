# Detroit: Become Human

## Overview

*Detroit: Become Human* is an interactive drama action-adventure game developed by Quantic Dream. The game is set in a futuristic Detroit where androids serve humans, but some begin to gain self-awareness and emotions. Players control three androids as they navigate moral choices that shape the story.

The game focuses on narrative and player choice. Every decision affects the plot, leading to multiple endings. The story explores themes of artificial intelligence, freedom, and what it means to be human.

Players experience the game from the perspectives of three androids: Kara, Connor, and Markus. Each character has a unique story and gameplay style, offering varied experiences throughout the game.

The game was praised for its graphics, emotional depth, and the branching storylines. It also received some criticism for occasionally stiff character animations and pacing issues.

*Detroit: Become Human* encourages players to reflect on ethical dilemmas, empathy, and the consequences of decisions in a near-future society.
